KORN

KORN

Gender Male
Occupation Band of Musicians
Voiced by Korn (themselves)
First Appearance Korn's Groovy Pirate Ghost Mystery

About

The only American metal band on record known to ride in a van and solve mysteries all over the country, Korn and their groovy sidekick Nibblet use their inept superpowers to solve mysteries and fight crime. Jonathan Davis, James Shaffer, Reginald Arvizu, David Silveria and Brian "Head" Welch stop by South Park one Halloween to investigate the mystery of the pirate ghosts. Together with the boys, they successfully unmask the true perpetrator.
